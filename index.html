At Bambu, we understand the uniqueness of each project and strive to offer customized solutions that meet our customers' specific requirements. Our bamboo products can be tailored to match any design or aesthetic preference.
We are dedicated to delivering resilient, eco-conscious materials that elevate your spaces with warmth, elegance, and long-lasting performance. Our Bambu Process
1. Choosing the Right Bamboo
We only harvest Moso bamboo that’s just right, not too young or too old (usually 5–7 years). This gives us the best strength and durability.
2. Cutting and Segregation
Each bamboo pole is carefully cut and sorted, ensuring only the highest quality sections are used in production.
3. Carbonization
We place the bamboo poles in the Carbonization chamber, where heat removes starch and sugar, preventing mold, pests, and warping.
4. Compression
The bamboo is compressed under high pressure and treated with anti-termite solution seven times, both on the top and bottom surfaces, for long-lasting protection.
